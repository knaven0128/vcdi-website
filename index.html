About Company

Visayas Coco Development Incorporated is a Philippine FDA licensed coconut oil manufacturer since 1993, with over 32 years of continuous production in Western Visayas. We supply crude coconut oil (CNO), refined coconut oil (RBD), and copra meal through an integrated farmer-trader network across Panay Island.


We are actively seeking trial supply partnerships with Global food manufacturers, distributors, and ingredient buyers.
